=== DELIVERY CONFIRMATION WEBHOOK ===
Add this AFTER "Send SMS" action in "Outgoing SMS (Webhook)" macro

curl -X POST \
  'https://nfd-rai.vercel.app/api/messages/delivery-confirmation' \
  -H 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  -d '{
  "phone": "{lv=customer_phone}",
  "message": "{lv=message_text}",
  "status": "delivered",
  "timestamp": "{time}"
}'

---

=== TRACK SENT SMS WEBHOOK ===
Use in "Track Sent SMS" macro to track YOUR manual messages

curl -X POST \
  'https://nfd-rai.vercel.app/api/messages/send' \
  -H 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  -d '{
  "conversationId": "lookup-by-phone",
  "customerPhone": "{sms_number}",
  "text": "{sms_body}",
  "sender": "staff",
  "trackOnly": true
}'

---
